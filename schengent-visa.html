Schengen Tourist Visa Requirements

To apply for a Schengen Tourist Visa, applicants must provide valid documentation that confirms their identity, purpose of visit, and financial stability. The following are the general requirements for most Schengen countries.

  • Valid passport with at least three months of validity after departure date.
  • Recent passport-size photographs as per embassy specifications.
  • Completed visa application form signed by the applicant.
  • Proof of travel arrangements and accommodation bookings.
  • Travel insurance covering a minimum of €30,000 within the Schengen area.
  • Proof of financial means such as bank statements or salary slips.
  • Employment certificate or business ownership proof.
  • Return flight reservation showing entry and exit dates.
