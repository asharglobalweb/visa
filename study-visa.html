Student & Study Visa Requirements

Applicants must provide valid academic and financial documentation proving eligibility and purpose of study. The following documents are commonly required for Schengen and UAE study visa applications.

  • Valid passport with at least six months of remaining validity.
  • Official admission letter from a recognized university or college.
  • Proof of payment for tuition or course registration fees.
  • Academic transcripts and educational certificates.
  • Proof of financial means to cover tuition and living expenses.
  • Medical insurance valid in the destination country.
  • Visa application form completed and signed by the applicant.
  • Passport-size photographs meeting embassy specifications.
