Privacy Policy

Jetset Visa Services respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our visa consultancy services. By accessing our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this carefully to understand our approach to handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary for providing visa consultancy and related services. This may include:

  • Full name, nationality, and contact details (email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Passport information and travel history
  • Employment or educational background (for specific visa categories)
  • Supporting documentation such as financial statements, residence proof, or invitation letters
  • Website usage data, IP address, and cookies for analytics and user experience improvement

How We Use Your Information

Jetset Visa Services uses your information only for legitimate business purposes related to visa consultation, documentation processing, and communication. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process visa applications and provide required consultancy services
  • Verify identity and eligibility for visa categories
  • Respond to inquiries, provide updates, and deliver requested information
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support experience
  • Comply with legal obligations, embassy requirements, and immigration regulations

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Embassies, consulates, or VFS centers for visa processing
  • Authorized service providers who assist in application handling or document verification
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights and comply with regulations

All third parties are obligated to maintain confidentiality and use your data only for the specific purpose for which it was shared.

Data Protection & Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. This includes encrypted storage, secure networks, and restricted access to personal information. While we take strong precautions, please note that no electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site performance. Cookies help us understand how visitors use our site and improve functionality. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some website features may not function properly as a result.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Once your data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Opt out of marketing or promotional communications
  • Request details on how your data is being used and stored

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Links to External Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Jetset Visa Services is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies when visiting such platforms.

Policy Updates

Jetset Visa Services reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated revision date. Continued use of our services after such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of the revised policy.
